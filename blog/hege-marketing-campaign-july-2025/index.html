Hege July Marketing Campaign

Starting July 12, HEGE is launching a coordinated marketing campaign designed to build momentum through the rest of the month and finally leave the $10M market cap behind us once and for all. Here’s what we have planned!

by Team Hege

Announcement / July 9, 2025

July Marketing Announcement

‍We’re combining real-world presence, digital ad reach, community rewards, and brand storytelling to put HEGE in front of new audiences, and remind the crypto world what we’re still building.

Let’s dive in!

1/10 – HEGE is going back to Vegas

‍Over four weekends, 10,000 HEGE sponsored maps will be handed out on the Vegas strip, putting our art, story, and brand directly into people’s hands.

To amplify the effect, we’ll also have walking billboards on the ground, real people repping HEGE, sparking curiosity, and pushing the brand in one of the world’s busiest tourist and leisure hubs. They will also be armed with Hege branded can holders and badges to hand out as prizes to people on the strip!

This isn’t our first time in Vegas, but it will be bigger, louder, and more coordinated than last time. It will also provide a bunch of great content for us to post across Reddit and other socials.

2/10 – DEX Screener Ads

Starting July 14, HEGE will run ad placements across DEX Screener – one of the most viewed sites in crypto. 100,000 impressions targeted at active traders. Every chart watcher will be seeing HEGE in the corner of their screen.

3/10 – Press Release Package‍

On Monday we will be publishing a press release package to amplify the brandcoin and merch narrative across 50+ crypto media outlets, including Associated Press, CoinMarketCap, Binance Feed and many more.

The release will spotlight HEGE’s story, vision, and community, building awareness as we scale. The hedgehog is making headlines!

4/10 – Sticker Competition

‍We want to see HEGE in all corners of the globe, and we’re rewarding the most creative, funny, and bold sticker placements around the world.

Prizes up to $250 for:

  • Best overall placement
  • Funniest placement
  • Most QR code scans (to be split between Hegends who can prove they were stickering in that city)

Order official stickers here or print your own: https://www.ebay.com/itm/176467248614 (for print-your-own, we recommend 3"x3" vinyl)

More details to be announced soon.

5/10 – CryptoTwits Trending Takeover

From Monday to Friday next week, we will be taking over CryptoTwits trending once again and rewarding top posters with up to $250 in prizes. Aiming for top exposure on and X reposts from their official accounts, HEGE will be impossible to ignore.

Create a FREE profile on CryptoTwits and add $HEGE.X to your watchlist today: https://stocktwits.com/symbol/HEGE.X

‍6/10 – X Raid Competition

‍Another X Raid Competition runs all next week from Monday through Friday. 20,000 HEGE will be distributed to the top 10 accounts on the leaderboard, as well as some of the top quality raiders (to be picked by the team). It’s simple: show up, raid, and get rewarded.

7/10 – Summer Merch Drop

We’re also dropping a new summer merch collection next week! If you’ve been watching the store, this will be a must-cop.

Check out the store here:

https://shop.hegecoin.com/

8/10 – Bitrue Alpha Listing

Bitrue has officially offered HEGE a listing on its brand new Alpha platform! Timing is still TBA, but it’s locked in and coming soon.

To celebrate, we’ll be teaming up with Bitrue to run some $HEGE giveaways and competitions for their customers. Let’s show them what Hege is all about!

9/10 – Live Shill Competitions

‍Next week we will be running live shill competitions during X Spaces. Up to $500 in prizes will be given out during the week to the best speakers who know how to pitch HEGE with energy, clarity, and conviction.

These won’t follow a fixed schedule, and will be announced ad-hoc as opportunities pop up. If you want in, keep a close eye on the Headquarters next week. Be sure to alert the chat if you find any opportunities!

10/10 – League of Hegends

Finally, we’re introducing a new initiative to make our community even stronger: the League of Hegends. Can you guess what it is!? (No, it’s not a game!)

In summary

To summarize, this is everything we have lined up for the coming week:

  • Vegas Campaign (10,000 branded maps)
  • DEX Screener Ads (100K impressions)
  • Press release package (50+ outlets)
  • Sticker competition ($250 in prizes or equivalent $HEGE)
  • CryptoTwits Competition ($250 in prizes or equivalent $HEGE)
  • X Raid Competition (20,000 $HEGE in prizes)
  • Summer Merch Drop
  • Bitrue Alpha Listing
  • League of Hegends

This is a coordinated push across multiple platforms, with rewards, online reach, and real-world presence.
