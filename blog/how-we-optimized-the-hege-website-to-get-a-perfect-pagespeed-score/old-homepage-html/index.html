The Underhog Story

Hege has always been a dreamer, but life hasn’t been kind to him. Orphaned at a young age after a tragic accident killed his parents, he grew up in hardship, fighting to survive in a world that never gave him a chance. Now lonely and shy, Hege has one burning ambition: to become a successful meme coin and finally win the heart of his crush, Hegena.

A Rival in the Way

But there’s a problem. Hegena already has a boyfriend, Hendrix—a wealthy, arrogant jock and heir to a billionaire hedge fund empire. Hendrix constantly belittles Hege, flaunting his riches and ridiculing Hege’s humble roots. To Hegena, Hendrix represents the life of luxury she craves, while Hege is little more than a struggling dreamer she barely notices. Winning her over seems impossible, but Hege has a plan…

A Meme With a Dream

Hege dives into the world of meme coins, channeling his passion into the creation of a project that’s all about him. He knows when his token hits a $1 billion market cap, he’ll be seen as a success not just by Hegena but by the entire world. The stakes are higher than ever for this underhog. Hege’s mission evolved from love to a redemption story, and proving that even an underhog can rise against all odds.

An Unexpected Ally

With Hegena currently out of the picture, her widowed mother, Helena, takes an interest in Hege. Much to his disgust, Helena flirts with Hege, offers encouragement, and is the only one who sees his true potential — and, at times, flirtatious hints that leave Hege more confused than ever. Even so, Hege’s focus remains steadfast on his ultimate goal: winning Hegena’s heart by becoming the meme of her dreams.

The Hege story features 4 characters each with their own quirks. With every new market cap milestone reached, more of their story is unlocked.

HEGE

We all love a good underdog story right? Hege’s persistent attempts at making ends meet, whilst feeling like the odds are stacked against him embodies a real go-getter attitude. He’s smart, ambitious and strong-willed—he’s paving the path for a life of wealth, authenticity and true hedgehog connection.

Hegena

Hegena is the calm in every bit of chaos. She’s kind and compassionate,
intelligent and confident, and despite her gravitation for keeping the peace, her spirit is bold and her voice certainly holds the attention of one Hegie in particular.

Hendrix

Hendrix is the opposite of slow to anger. Based out of spite and jealousy, his determination is shown by his relentless quest to ruin anything and everything that could get in the way of his social status. Hendrix may have the girl, the car, and social profile but how long can it be sustained when the ugliness of hatred gets unveiled?

Helena

Helena is the definition of fierce mixed with sass. She’s a force to be reckoned with and nobody’s fool. From putting the world right with her besties over a glass of red, championing her self-appointed role as neighbourhood watch-hog, and being a steady figure in Hegena’s life—it’s Helena’s world and we’re all just living in it.
