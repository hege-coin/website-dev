To bounce, or not to bounce?

In the markets, everything bounced this week.

The market bounced back strongly this week already, and it seems dip buyers stepped in earlier than expected. This could lead to a renewed rally into the end of the year.

Bitcoin followed the stock market up, continuing the bounce from the low around $81k. If tech stocks keep rallying, it could well continue higher. However, remember that unlike tech stocks, bitcoin properly broke down the week before last, so technically it looks much much weaker.

After the breakdown in bitcoin, we should expect there to be a lot of people still looking to de-risk their crypto holdings. So until there are clear signs of a bottom being in, any pump should be viewed with suspicion.
