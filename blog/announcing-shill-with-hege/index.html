Shill With HEGE: Come Tell Us What You're Building!

Introducing a new series of Hege X spaces for all creators in the crypto world. Whatever you’re building, join us and share what you’re working on!

by Day

Announcement / February 28, 2026

A New X Space For All Creators in Crypto

This coming Wednesday, on March 4th, we’ll be holding our first Shill With HEGE — a new series of X spaces all about creators in the crypto world.

Hosted by Hege OGs @plantfaceash and @itsdayagain, every week three invited guests will come on and speak about what they’re building, and what makes them excited to work in the crypto space.

You’ll get to hear from the real people, down in the trenches. The ones that put up a fat middle finger to the shitty market we currently find ourselves in, and just keep grinding to make their visions come alive.

This is where you get the real alpha. So be there or be bear, and read on for all the details!

A One Hour Space, Every Wednesday

So this is how it’s gonna go down: every Wednesday at 9 AM UTC, Ash and Day will sit down, catch up a little bit, and then dive into the main section of the show, which is going to be our invited guests.

(Yes, we know we know, this is a weird timeslot for all you crypto yanks. We love you but time zones are a bitch, so this is what you get.)

Anyway there will be three guests per show. And no, it won’t be just memecoins, but we’ll cover the whole spectrum: memes, utility coins, NFTs, brands, artists, analysts — really just anybody who is building something interesting that they want to share.

Each invited guest gets exactly 4 minutes to pitch their thing, and then another 4 minutes to answer questions from the hosts. That’s 8 minutes in total. No more, no less. (And yea, we’ll be strict with this, so prepare accordingly!)

Then before the end of the hour, we’ll open up the floor for 15 minutes, and let anybody in the audience up to ask questions or share their thoughts.

Thanks for Reading

You don’t want to miss this space, so see you on Wednesday!

And as usual a big shout out to the entire Hege Community: you’re 100% awesome.

Make sure to stop by our Telegram Hegequarters and say hi if you’re not in there already. It’s the best place to get the latest on what’s going on. Good vibes guaranteed.

Also don’t miss the next TA Tuesday livestream to stay up to date on what’s going on in the markets. Every Tuesday at 17:00 UTC, live in our Telegram.

That’s it. Until next time,

— Team Hege
