Rate cut, incoming!

On Wednesday the Fed’s FOMC, chaired by head-hog Powell, is expected to cut rates another 25 basis points, bringing the target rate down to 3.5-3.75%. This is generally considered bullish for risk assets like stocks and crypto.

Stocks were slightly up on the week, making a continued rally into the end of the year seem likely.

Meanwhile, crypto ended the week flat, the bounce selling off again. Like we’ve been saying over the past two weeks now, this is what we expect is going to keep happen. The bitcoin breakdown dealt heavy technical damage to the chart, and that’s probably going to take time to repair. No matter what influencers on X would like to have you believe.

We’ll keep monitoring the charts every week of course for signs of a bottom being in, but we do have to be prepared for it to take time.
